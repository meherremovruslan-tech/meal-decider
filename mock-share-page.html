Chicken Biryani
Ingredients
• 500g chicken thighs, cut into pieces
• 2 cups basmati rice
• 2 large onions, thinly sliced
• 3 tomatoes, chopped
• 4 cloves garlic, minced
• Biryani spice mix ⚠ may need to buy
• Saffron strands ⚠ may need to buy
• Fresh mint & coriander ⚠ may need to buy
• 3 tbsp ghee or butter ⚠ may need to buy
---
Steps
1. Marinate the chicken — Mix chicken with yogurt, garlic, and half the spices. Rest 30 min.
2. Fry the onions — Cook onions in ghee until deep golden brown. Set half aside.
3. Cook the base — Fry remaining onions with spices 1 min. Add marinated chicken, cook 15 min.
4. Par-boil the rice — Boil rice with whole spices to 70% done. Drain.
5. Layer & dum — Layer rice over chicken. Add saffron milk and fried onions. Cover tightly, cook on low 20 min.
6. Rest & serve — Rest 5 min before opening. Mix gently and garnish with fresh herbs.
---
Tips
Use day-old cooked rice if possible — it holds up better in the dum step without turning mushy.
Don't skip the resting time — the trapped steam finishes the rice perfectly without burning the bottom.
