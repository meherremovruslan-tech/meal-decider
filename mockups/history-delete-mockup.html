🍽️ Editable Meal History — Mockup

Two ways the delete could feel. Both add a small × next to every meal (it turns red when you point at it). Try clicking the × buttons below — they work!
The same design would apply to both the “Recent Meals” card on the main page and the “Meal History” card on the profile page.

Option A — Ask before deleting
Tapping × turns the row into a small red confirmation. Nothing is deleted until “Delete” is pressed. Safest, but two taps every time.
Recent Meals 4
Option B — Delete instantly, with Undo
Tapping × removes the meal immediately, and an “Undo” message appears for 5 seconds. One tap, mistakes are recoverable.
