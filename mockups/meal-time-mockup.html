How the smart default works: when the page opens, the chip matching the clock is already selected and carries a small AUTO badge. The hint line underneath spells it out: “✨ We picked Lunch for you — it’s midday. Tap another to change.” The moment the user taps a different chip, the AUTO badge disappears and the hint switches to a plain confirmation (“Dinner it is. The AI will suggest dinner meals.”), so it never feels like the app is overriding them. Tapping the selected chip again deselects it (hint: “No preference — the AI will suggest any kind of meal.”).

Time windows: 04:00–11:00 → Breakfast · 11:00–16:00 → Lunch · 16:00–04:00 → Dinner.
